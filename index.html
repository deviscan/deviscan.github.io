About Us

Devi Scan with “quality & service” as its motto aims at providing ultra sound scan facility by highly qualified, experienced & thoroughly professional radiologist using sophisticated, state–of–the-art equipments and infrastructure with personal attention, extreme patient care, friendly supportive staff and prompt yet perfect reports.

Devi Scan was started in 9th December 2016 with the ultimate motive of providing quality ultrasonography & Doppler facilities by experienced, highly qualified senior radiologist which infact appears to be the need of the hour!!!

The diagnostic facility in its present premises was inaugurated by stalwarts & doyens from the medical fraternity and the field of Radiology. Dr. P. Ramakrishnan, MD, DLO, Former Dean,Government Kilpauk Medical College & Controller of Examination, Bharath University, And Prof. T.S. Swaminathan, MD, DMRD, FICR., Former Director of Barnard Institute of Radiology, Rajiv Gandhi General Hospital, Madras Medical College, Chennai & HOD – Radiology, Dr. Kamakshi Memorial Hospital, Velachery.
